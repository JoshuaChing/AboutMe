Who am I?


Born in Toronto, I spent majority of my time growing up in Markham. Graduated from Pierre Elliott Trudeau High School, I am currently studying in the Systems Design Engineering co-op program at the University of Waterloo.

My journey as a programmer began in grade 9 where I was first exposed to the idea of coding. This grew into a passion to apply my thought process as an engineer to the complexity of programming. I aspire to design and develop applications that will solve every day challenges and make living simple.

Asides from engineering, I also enjoy playing a variety of instruments including the guitar, drums, and ukulele. In high school I breakdanced and was a founder of the Pierre Elliott Trudeau Dance Team. I also had the joy of being on the high school Ultimate Frisbee Team.

I would love to try new things and work with others who share the same passions as me. If you would like to contact me, please feel free to e-mail me at jyching@uwaterloo.ca
